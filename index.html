SIMPCRAFT

Welcome to the Simpcraft Minecraft Server Wiki.

Simpcraft is a semi-anarchy server created by Plutoren for Mr. Beast’s raid video shown here. The server came up on July 28th 2020 as a paper minecraft server.

Explore SimpcraftPlay Now!

A Brief Overview of the Simpcraft Minecraft Server History.

Pre-Video Period

In the beginning, there was about a 30-40 player playerbase. It consisted of very few toxic players. Overall, the community ... was a lot more modest. Plutoren said that if server members could plant 200k trees ingame that a /spawn command would be added. During this time there were two main groups, the beastmc builders and the kelp clan. The kelp clan was the first clan on the server made by rockyflyer. Its main base was griefed later on causing the clan to fade into nothing. During this, the beastmc builders were created. The plan was to make Spawn 2.0. a new spawn built by them for Mr. Beast. During this, Spawnville, a community base started at -1000x, -1000z, later it moved to -13000x, 10000z due to several grief attempts. Other than that, not a large number of things happened in the pre-video period.

Congested / Post Congested Period

The congested period was an interesting time. The video that MrBeast ran the raids for released. Large amounts of players ... flocked to the server to see it for themselves In the beginning, a large number of OG's went to spawn to stop new players from joining. There was one fixed spawn at the time, so they filled it with lava and surrounded it with obsidian. Despite doing this, the players escaped from the trap, and large amounts of players fleed from spawn. The server tps had gone down. Players that had been on the server before the video had gone out rushed to defend their bases. During this time a large number of the well-known players joined including Tech1e, flowers101, and Gune. A few days after the video went out, people started hating on the beastmc builders for being partially responsible for the original spawn trap and for wanting spawn protection on "Spawn 2.0". As a result "Spawn 2.0" was griefed to oblivion. While this was going on, Gune ruled over the spawn and flowers101 camped the end so traveling near spawn was dangerous. Plutoren also took the discord server from rockyflyer and did a vote on it to see if people wanted certain features. In the end, people voted against almost every feature. Right before the congested period ended the clan ROB was created. ROB consisted of three members, rockyflyer, flowers101, and Tech1e. The Post-Congested Period lasted from August 16th to December 22nd. During this time groups were made and groups fell. Simpcraft went up and down in popularity hitting a max of about 40 players during the max time. One big group that sprang up was balance They were very powerful and had one of the biggest bases on the server at the time. In general not a lot happened during this time. Groups looked into preserving spawn, although it was later decided against.

The New Times

As this era started just after the book dupe the server was somewhat split between those that duped and those that did not ... it took a long time for items to trickle down to those that did not dupe. As time progressed the server had some extremely low player counts these low player counts made it possible for projects to take place at spawn Player counts did not stay low forever though as new players started to pour into the server and some players that had quite decided to come back. Many bases where made and fell in this time of new life in the server. At some point after this a new philosophy emerged in some players involving the destruction of every and all builds that they had cords to or could find.
