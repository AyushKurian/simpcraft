Sodatopia Timeline

Sodatopia was located at 30 1700 meaning that the majority of players leaving spawn would pass by it.
This lead to a massive amount of players joining it in its lifetime.

The Beginning
The Start

Sodatopia was started by sodapop107 as a rest stop.

Close to Spawn
Not Far

Sodatopia, as you would expect seeing its proximity to spawn, people would grief nonstop.

Sodatopia Destroyed
How it Happened

Sodatopia suffered griefs on a weekly basis and was abandoned a few days later.

Present Day

Lasted from November 10th till November 30th.it quickly grew to be way more.
