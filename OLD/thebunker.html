History of The Bunker

The Bunker was a group base founded by Declipsonator on July 29th 2020. The base was constructed by Declipsonator, ClancyH, and irexclone. Since the base started so early on, it was very close to spawn because the world border was only 30k by 30k at the time. The bunker was completely built underground, but was not that secure. The first problem with it was the nether portal. It was thought to not be necessary to put out nether portal far away. As a result the base was griefed later on as a result. Second the chests inside were not being used in a natural formation so any player with storage esp on could easily see the base. In the end, the base was griefed by bulanpepita.
