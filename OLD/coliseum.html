History of The Coliseum

The Coliseum was one of the most impressive and large buildings close to the spawn. The Coliseum (Colo as the players would call it) was a large obsidian structure. Before the Colo actually came into existence a project that involved nuking spawn to bedrock was in the process. The Colo was built on top of the bedrock with extremely tall pillars going up to sea level. It was an arena specifically for crystalling and being a meeting place for people across the server. Along with the Obsidian the building was also decorated and embossed with bits of crying obsidian (a rare resource that had not been duped). Probably the most important part of the build is that in the middle of the arena area of the Colo was the original bedrock that Mr. Beast placed at the beginning of his video where the server was born. The Coliseum was built by many members of the Alliance (a group of many players including Balance and Brazil). The Colo was built quickly due to the size of the Alliance and the people willing to help. Nobody with power would grief the Colo because it had brought people together and was a nice meeting place. But as turmoil between several players and a person named H34r7 continued, H3 decided to wither the Coliseum. One morning H3 spawned over 50 withers on the Colo with the reason being “idk I felt like it”. Though that was far from the truth, with H3’s past experiences with other players he subconsciously wanted revenge. This would begin the largest war of the server’s history, which in turn broke the Alliance. After that, the Colo was a wasteland and now a griefed building. One rebuild was attempted but Big_Chunky69 griefed it .The Colo would not be rebuilt until many weeks afterwards. The Colo was rebuilt by several players who wanted it back. It would soon be griefed again by several other griefers who didn’t like the builders. The Colo was rebuilt two other times after that with the final grief having a large lava cast over it. The lava cast is still on the Coliseum (May 28, 2021).
