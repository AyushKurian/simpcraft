SIMPCRAFT

Welcome to the SimpCraft Minecraft Server Wiki.

What is Simpcraft?

SimpCraft is a semi-anarchy server created by Plutoren for Mr. Beast’s raid video shown here. The server came up on July 28th 2020 as a paper minecraft server with it’s only intent to be a raid server for Mr. Beast. At the time of filming they got more than 1000 people to join the server within a minute.

Remains of the video can still be found at -240 -240 as the bedrock placed by the Mr. Beast crew is still there. After the video ended Plutoren kept the server up for fun. This was one of the main reasons SimpCraft is what it is today.

The Pre-Video Period

In the beginning, there was about a 30-40 player playerbase. It consisted of very few toxic players. Overall, the community was a lot more modest. Plutoren said that if server members could plant 200k trees ingame that a /spawn command would be added. During this time there were two main groups, the beastmc builders and the kelp clan.

The kelp clan was the first clan on the server made by rockyflyer. Its main base was griefed later on causing the clan to fade into nothing. During this, the beastmc builders were created. The plan was to make Spawn 2.0. a new spawn built by them for Mr. Beast. During this, Spawnville, a community base started at -1000x, -1000z, later it moved to -13000x, 10000z due to several grief attempts. Other than that, not a large number of things happened in the pre-video period.

The Congested Period

The congested period was an interesting time. The video that MrBeast ran the raids for released. Large amounts of players flocked to the server to see it for themselves. In the beginning, a large number of OG's went to spawn to stop new players from joining. There was one fixed spawn at the time, so they filled it with lava and surrounded it with obsidian. Despite doing this, the players escaped from the trap, and large amounts of players fleed from spawn. The server tps had gone down. Players that had been on the server before the video had gone out rushed to defend their bases. During this time a large number of the well-known players joined including Tech1e, flowers101, and Gune.

A few days after the video went out, people started hating on the beastmc builders for being partially responsible for the original spawn trap and for wanting spawn protection on "Spawn 2.0". As a result "Spawn 2.0" was griefed to oblivion. While this was going on, Gune ruled over the spawn and flowers101 camped the end so traveling near spawn was dangerous. Plutoren also took the discord server from rockyflyer and did a vote on it to see if people wanted certain features. In the end, people voted against almost every feature. Right before the congested period ended the clan ROB was created. ROB consisted of three members, rockyflyer, flowers101, and Tech1e.
